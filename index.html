Brazil Summit Europe

23 – 24 April 2022

A two-day conference that will reunite political leaders, entrepreneurs, civil society actors, and academics to discuss Brazilian public policies at the Hertie School in Berlin. What can we learn from each other? How can we enhance cooperation and what are the underlying opportunities?
The Summit is open to the public and live interpretation in English and Portuguese will be available. Participation is possible both onsite and online. Register now!

Why a Brazilian Summit in Europe?

Coming from a strong political polarization, Brazil will face presidential elections at the end of 2022, a crucial moment to impact the country’s agenda. Our aim is to foster discussions and exchange experiences between Brazilian and European leaders, politicians, and academics.

Planed Discussions

Brazil Summit Europe will bring to Berlin leading actors from the private and public sectors across diversified panel discussions that will address different policy issues.

The Summit will cover

  • Trending Brazilian industries for foreign investment
  • Digital governance policies
  • Universal access to health care and the Covid-19 crisis management
  • Innovations on sustainability and the environmental agenda
  • The promotion of democratic values.
