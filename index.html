Brazil Summit Europe

23 – 24 April 2022

A two-day conference that will bring together Brazilian and European leaders at the Hertie School in Berlin to debate the current state of Brazilian public policies through a bilateral European & Brazilian perspective. What can we learn from each other? How can we enhance cooperation and what are the underlying opportunities?

REGISTER

Why a Brazilian Summit in Europe?

Coming from a strong political polarization, Brazil will face presidential elections at the end of 2022, a crucial moment to impact the country’s agenda. Our aim is to foster discussions and exchange experiences between Brazilian and European leaders, politicians, and academics.

Planed Discussions

Brazil Summit Europe will bring to Berlin leading actors from the private and public sectors across five panel discussions that will address different policy issues.

The Summit will cover

  • Trending Brazilian industries for foreign investment
  • Digital governance policies
  • Universal health care and the Covid-19 crisis management
  • Innovations on sustainability and the environmental agenda
  • The promotion of democratic values.
